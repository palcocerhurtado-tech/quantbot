# Backlinks and Local SEO Playbook - Archon Consultancies

## Objective

Build authority around:

- `consultoria de ia`
- `agencia de ia`
- `automatizacion con ia`

while reinforcing local trust signals for Zaragoza and Spain.

## 1. Google Business Profile

Official setup:

- https://business.google.com/us/business-profile/
- https://support.google.com/business/answer/7039811?hl=en

Use this business description:

`Archon Consultancies ayuda a empresas y pymes a implantar automatizacion, agentes y sistemas con IA para reducir trabajo manual, conectar herramientas y ganar control operativo. Trabajamos con negocios que quieren escalar con procesos mas rapidos, menos friccion y mejor visibilidad.`

Suggested services to load into the profile:

- Consultoria de IA
- Agencia de IA
- Automatizacion con IA
- Automatizacion de procesos
- Agentes de IA para empresas
- Auditoria operativa

First photo set to upload:

- Logo
- Hero screenshot of the homepage
- Screenshot of `consultoria-ia.html`
- Screenshot of `agencia-ia.html`
- Screenshot of `automatizacion-ia-empresas.html`

Posts to publish in the first 30 days:

- `Que automatizar primero en una pyme`
- `Diferencia entre consultoria de IA y agencia de IA`
- `Como reducir tareas manuales con automatizacion con IA`
- `Errores comunes al implantar IA en empresas`

## 2. Bing Places and Apple Business Connect

Official setup:

- https://www.bingplaces.com/
- https://businessconnect.apple.com/
- https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-business-connect/overview-of-apple-business-connect-abcb205640e7/web

Action:

- Claim the business in Bing Places.
- Claim the business in Apple Business Connect.
- Use the same business name, website, email, and service-area wording everywhere.

## 3. NAP and entity consistency

Use this exact business identity everywhere:

- Name: `Archon Consultancies`
- Website: `https://archonconsultancies.com/`
- Contact email: `auditoria@archonconsultancies.com`
- Service area: `Zaragoza, Espana`
- Core services: `Consultoria de IA`, `Agencia de IA`, `Automatizacion con IA`

## 4. Review acquisition

Send this message to past clients or warm contacts:

`Hola [Nombre], estoy reforzando la presencia digital de Archon Consultancies y una resena honesta sobre el trabajo de automatizacion/IA que hicimos juntos me ayudaria mucho. Si te encaja, te paso el enlace para dejarla. Gracias.`

Review angle prompts:

- Claridad de la auditoria
- Capacidad para detectar procesos manuales
- Rapidez de implementacion
- Impacto en operaciones
- Reduccion de tiempo o errores

## 5. Backlink priorities

Priority 1:

- Client case studies with a link from the client website
- Technology partner directories
- Founder profiles on LinkedIn with page links
- Local business associations or chambers

Priority 2:

- Guest posts on operations, ecommerce, automation, and SME media
- Podcast appearances with show notes links
- Startup and consulting directories

Priority 3:

- Curated agency listings
- Spanish business directories that are indexed and clean

`Caso de automatizacion conjunto`

Body:

## 7. Internal content cadence

Publish one new page or article every 2 weeks around:

- `consultoria de ia para pymes`
- `agencia de ia para empresas`
- `automatizacion con ia para ecommerce`
- `agentes de ia para soporte`
- `automatizacion administrativa con ia`

## 8. Link routing

Always route links like this:

- Broad brand or mixed intent -> home
- Strategy and diagnosis intent -> `consultoria-ia.html`
- Execution and implementation intent -> `agencia-ia.html`
- Process optimization intent -> `automatizacion-ia-empresas.html`

- Claim Google Business Profile
- Claim Bing Places
- Claim Apple Business Connect
- Submit sitemap in Google Search Console
- Submit sitemap in Bing Webmaster Tools
- Request 5 reviews
- Ask 3 clients or partners for backlinks
